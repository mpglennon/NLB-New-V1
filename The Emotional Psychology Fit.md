## The Risk: Ambiguity in "Never Look Back"

For someone in acute financial stress—your middle manager facing layoff anxiety—the phrase could trigger **avoidance behavior** rather than healthy forward focus. The psychology of emotional resilience requires **acknowledging reality while maintaining optimism**, not bypassing difficulty.
